Features

Accredited

We are an accredited provider of National Criminal History Checks with Australian Criminal Intelligence Commission (ACIC).

Secure

We are committed to data security and review and audit our software and service delivery infrastructure consistently to ensure industry best practice.

Cost effective

No set up fees or administration costs. We train you, conduct audits and provide customer service as part of your account. You will only pay for the checks you do.

Nationwide

As providers of National Criminal History checks, results are returned from all states, regardless of location of applicant.

Fast

80% of Criminal History Checks are returned in two days and most checks have an immediate outcome. Complete your first check in minutes.

100% Online

Our web-based software is accessible anywhere, anytime – all you need is internet connection.

Customer focused

We have a highly experienced customer support team to provide phone support and step-by-step training to facilitate the smooth running of your checks.

Flexible

We have a range of payment options tailored to reduce costs

Caring

We seek to understand the needs of your organisation to optimise your check results.
