What we do

Organisations

National criminal History checks

We are an accredited agency with the National Criminal Intelligence Commission, we provide web based portals to organisations to allow them to control the Criminal History Checks that they submit. Our primary aim is to provide fast, secure and affordable checks that are accurate and appropriate to each organisations individual needs.

International Criminal History Checks:

We have agreements with several sources to supply International Criminal History Checks from various court systems. As with national criminal history checks our aim is to provide the most accurate and appropriate reports for your organisation’s needs.

Individual

We can conduct National Criminal History Checks for individuals, feel free to contact us during business hours so that we can discuss your check and the best type of check to meet your needs.

There are a variety of descriptions that you can utilise for a Police Check, we would prefer to understand why you need the check so that we can provide value for money and you don’t end up with a Criminal History Check that is rejected by employers.
