Thanks Crimcheck

CrimCheck has improved our criminal history checking systems enormously. Most of our checks are back within 1 to 3 minutes. This is an astonishing improvement on 21 days from our previous provider.Bernie, St. John Ambulance

Bernie, St. John Ambulance

We provide a fast, secure and affordable online submission service for criminal history checks Australia-wide via the National Police Database.

Criminal history checking as part of the recruitment process is essential for risk mitigation for all organisations.

CrimCheck are committed to providing your organisation with professional service, support and most importantly accurate results.

Above all we believe in making a difference to our community, and all our profits are donated to South East Volunteers who provide the local community with social support services.
